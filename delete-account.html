Privacy Policy — Earn in home

Last updated: Apr 17, 2026

Earn in home (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

Information we collect

  • Photos / Images (User-provided): If you choose to take a picture in the app, we collect the image you capture.
  • App activity and diagnostics: We collect information about how the app is used and crash/performance diagnostics to improve stability and user experience.
  • Device identifiers: Certain device identifiers may be collected for security, analytics, and advertising/measurement purposes.

Camera permission

We request camera access only to allow you to take pictures inside the app. The camera is not used in the background.

How we use your information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and operate app features (including features that require taking and uploading photos)
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse
  • Support analytics and advertising/measurement (where enabled)

Uploading and storage of photos

Photos you capture may be uploaded to our servers to provide the feature you requested (for example, profile or verification features). We store photos only as needed to operate the service and support requested functionality.

Sharing and third‑party services

We may use third‑party SDKs (such as analytics and advertising services). These providers may collect and process certain information according to their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the app and for legitimate business purposes (such as security, compliance, and dispute resolution).

Your choices and deletion

You can delete your uploaded photos and/or your account from inside the app. If you need help, contact us.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure.

Contact us
