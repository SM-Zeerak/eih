Account & data deletion

For the mobile application Earn In Home (listed on Google Play; developer product “Earn in home”).

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This page explains how to ask us to delete your Earn In Home account and associated personal data tied to that account.

How to request account deletion

  • Email. Send a message from the email address registered on your account to support@earninhome.com with the subject Earn In Home — Account deletion request. Include your registered email and, if you know them, your user ID or refer ID so we can confirm ownership.

What we delete

Once we verify your request, we delete or anonymize, where technically feasible:

  • Your account profile information (such as name, email on file, and profile photo if stored with us).
  • Credentials and identifiers used only for your account (sessions/tokens invalidated as part of deletion where applicable).
  • App activity tied to your user identifier (such as rewards/points ledger, referrals, and task submissions—as applicable).

What we may retain

  • Legal and security: information we must keep to meet legal obligations, prevent abuse, enforce our terms, or handle disputes.
  • Backups and aggregates: residual copies may exist in backups for a limited time before routine rotation; we do not use retained data for new purposes incompatible with deletion.

Processing time

We acknowledge requests promptly and aim to finish deletion within 30 days unless a lawful exception requires a longer retention. We confirm by email once processing is complete.

Partial deletion

If you need specific data removed without closing your entire account (for example profile photo only), reply to our support email explaining what to remove. If we cannot honor a partial request, we will tell you whether full account deletion is required.

For how we collect and use data generally, see the Privacy Policy.
